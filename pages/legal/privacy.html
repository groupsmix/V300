Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Terms Privacy About FAQ Contact Support Center
📅 Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: email, display name, and group submissions. We also collect usage data including page views, search queries, and interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and improve our services, process payments, send notifications, prevent abuse, and generate aggregate statistics.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. We implement Row-Level Security (RLS) to protect user data. Some data is cached locally in your browser using localStorage.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage for: theme preferences, saved groups, recently viewed groups, and session caching. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Third-Party Services

We use: Supabase (database), Cloudflare (hosting & analytics), and LemonSqueezy (payments). Each has its own privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

You can: access your data, request deletion, update your profile, and opt out of communications. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at our contact page or visit the Support Center.
